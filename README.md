# GST Invoice Generator

A clean, free, no-watermark GST Invoice Generator that works 100% offline after first load.

## Features
-  CGST + SGST (same state) vs IGST (different state) — auto-detected
-  HSN/SAC codes for each line item
-  Professional PDF invoice download (no watermark)
-  Saves your business details in browser (no account needed)
-  Amount in words (Indian number system — Lakh, Crore)
-  Tax summary breakdown by GST rate
-  Works 100% offline after first load
-  Auto-save draft to browser

## How to Use
1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
2. Fill in your business details (saved automatically)
3. Add your client details
4. Add line items with HSN/SAC codes and GST rates
5. Click **Download PDF** to get your invoice
